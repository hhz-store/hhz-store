iPhone App Guide

Add HHZ Store to your iPhone home screen for a faster, app-like experience. Open instantly with one tap — no downloads needed.

1

Open Safari

Launch Safari on your iPhone and go to hhz-store.github.io/hhz-store

🌐
2

Tap the Share button

At the bottom of the Safari screen, tap the Share icon (square with an upward arrow).

⬆️
3

Scroll & tap "Add to Home Screen"

In the share sheet, scroll down past the app icons and tap "Add to Home Screen" (square with a + icon).

➕
4

Tap "Add"

You can edit the name if you like, then tap "Add" in the top-right corner. HHZ will be added to your home screen.

✅
5

Launch from Home Screen

Find the HHZ icon on your home screen. Tap it anytime to open the store instantly — just like a real app!

Web Guide

Policies

📦

Shipping Policy

We offer free worldwide shipping on all orders. Estimated delivery time is 8–15 business days depending on your location. All orders are processed within 24–48 hours of placement. Tracking information will be provided via WhatsApp once your order ships.

🔄

Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement at no charge. After 24 hours, the order enters processing and cancellation may not be possible. To request a cancellation, message us on WhatsApp with your order ID.

📋

Refund Policy

All sales are final. No refunds are accepted after the order has been placed and confirmed, except in the following cases:

  • The package has not arrived within 30 business days of the estimated delivery window
  • The product arrived with missing parts, damaged components, or incorrect items

If your order qualifies for a refund under these conditions, please contact us via WhatsApp with your order ID and clear photos of the issue. Refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days after verification.

🔒

Privacy Policy

Your personal information (name, address, phone number) is collected solely for the purpose of processing and delivering your order. We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties. Your payment details are processed securely and are not stored on our servers.

💬

Contact & Support

For any questions, concerns, or claims regarding your order, reach out to us on WhatsApp. We aim to respond within 2–4 hours during business hours. Please have your order ID ready for faster assistance.
